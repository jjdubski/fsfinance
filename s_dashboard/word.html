First Step Finance

Stock

A

stock

(also known as equity) is a security that represents the ownership of a fraction of a corporation.

Example(s):

If a company has 1000 shares of

stock

outstanding and one person owns 100 shares, that person would own and have claim to 10% of the company's assets and earnings.

Between 1982 and 1986 the value of the company's

stock

rose by 86%.
