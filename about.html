Our Company

First Step Finance LLC was founded in March 2022 in Illinois. Our founders felt the need to do something about the lack of financial education in schools. Since then our team has been working hard to provide students with a cutting-edge curriculum and technology.

Our Tech

We use a full curriculum that teaches students about budgeting and finance, as well as many other forms of updated education. Our curriculum spans multiple classes and we use social media to help make interactable experiences for students.
