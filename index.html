Welcome to First Step Finance

Empowering students and teachers with the knowledge to make informed financial decisions.

Learn More

Teenagers and young adults lack knowledge of fundamental financial literacy skills across the Chicagoland area and suburbs. This in turn leads to an increase in substandard spending habits and improper debt taken on by these young adults. Our solution at First Step Finance is a supplemental user-friendly customizable workbook that targets the three pillars of financial literacy: Banking, Budgeting, and Investing, within the target audience for the company. The goal of this is to spread healthy financial practices within the youth of a community so that they gain wealth rather than just income. Education allows a break in the poverty cycle, and overall the goal of First Step Finance is to provide that education at an affordable and accessible cost to these students.

Whether you are a high school student, in college, or just looking to improve your financial literacy, we can assist you at all levels. Our programs are able to serve a large spectrum of students, from complete beginners to confident financial decision-makers. We want to provide education to those who are looking to take their financial literacy further. Begin by taking your first step today.
